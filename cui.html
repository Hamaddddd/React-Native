COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) started its
journey in 1998, and established its first campus at
Islamabad in April 1998. In August 2000,
in recognition of CUI's achievements, the
Federal Government granted it the status of a
Degree Awarding Institute (DAI)
through promulgation of its charter.
